How To Play

Start of the Game

  • Click 'Start Game' when all players have joined

Swapping Cards

  • Click on a card in your hand that you'd like to swap
  • Click on a card in the "You" section that you'd like to swap your hand card with
  • Repeat for as many cards as you want to swap
  • Click "Done" when you've finished

Playing Cards

  • To play one card, click the card you would like to play
  • Next, click the card on top of the deck to play your card
  • To play multiple cards, click all the cards you would like to play
  • Next, click the card on top of the deck to play your cards

Picking up

  • If you can't play and need to pick up, click the card on top of the deck
